How Does Starlink Work?

The Starlink satellite dish is installed outside with a clear view of the sky. A cable runs from the dish into your home (through a wall, attic, crawlspace, etc.) and connects to the router inside the home, which powers the kit and broadcasts Wi-Fi throughout the house.
